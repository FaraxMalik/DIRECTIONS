# My Directions 🧭

**Your personal guide to discovering who you are and where you're headed.**

My Directions is a career guidance app that helps you understand yourself better through personality assessments and connects you with career paths that truly fit who you are.

## What's Inside

- **Personality Insights**: Take scientifically-backed tests (MBTI & Big Five) to understand your traits, preferences, and strengths
- **Smart Career Suggestions**: Get personalized career recommendations based on your unique personality profile
- **AI Career Counselor**: Chat with Faraz, your AI career advisor, anytime you need guidance or have questions
- **Personal Journal**: Reflect on your journey with a beautiful vintage-style journal
- **Career Quiz**: Answer questions about your interests and get tailored career paths

## Why My Directions?

Choosing a career path shouldn't feel like guessing in the dark. My Directions combines personality science with AI to help you make informed decisions about your future—ones that align with who you really are.

## Tech Stack

Built with Flutter and powered by:
- Firebase (Authentication & Storage)
- Google Gemini AI (Personality Analysis & Career Matching)
- Groq AI (Conversational Career Counseling)

## Getting Started

1. Clone this repository
2. Run `flutter pub get`
3. Add your Firebase configuration files (`google-services.json` for Android, `GoogleService-Info.plist` for iOS)
4. Set up API keys:
   - Copy `lib/config/api_keys.dart.example` to `lib/config/api_keys.dart`
   - Add your actual API keys (get them from [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/) for Gemini and [Groq Console](https://console.groq.com/) for Groq)
5. Run `flutter run`

## Design Philosophy

The app features a warm crimson and beige aesthetic, inspired by classic journals and vintage stationery—because your career journey deserves to feel personal and thoughtful, not clinical.
